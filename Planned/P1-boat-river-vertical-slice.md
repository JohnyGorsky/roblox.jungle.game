# P1 — Boat + river vertical slice

**Source:** Job 001 roadmap. **Depends:** P0.

A **playable greybox prototype** (placeholder art) to answer one question: **does driving the boat
feel good?** Not the final look — the real boat (Meshy) + polish come in P9, dropped onto these same
mechanics.

## Scope
- A **winding** river stretch (curved, never straight) built with **sculpted terrain** for the
  banks/island; placeholder water. Bends/narrows to steer around.
- A **boat controller** — drive / steer / throttle, tuned so following the river's curves feels good;
  a chase camera that works on mobile.
- **Mobile-first controls** from day one (touch joystick/throttle), mapping to PC keys too.
- Placeholder boat = a simple block/part; no combat, roles, or fuel yet (those are P2–P4).

## Success test
- Steering the winding river feels responsive and fun with a placeholder block.
- Runs on a phone-sized viewport with touch controls.

→ Promote to a job.
