🛠️ Dev Notes — Day/night & land threats

🌅 A day/night cycle now runs — and it changes where the danger is.
🐊 Crocs (water) get deadlier at NIGHT; 🐆 panthers (land) prowl the banks and hit hardest by DAY.
🏝️ Panthers ambush from shore as you pass — they can't chase across open water like crocs.
🛶 You now spawn on the boat (no more falling through the world), and downed players stay put.
🎨 Greybox threats + basic day/night for now; night atmosphere, fog & real animals come at the art pass.
