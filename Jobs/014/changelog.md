🛠️ Dev Notes — Mounted gun

🔫 The boat has a mounted gun at the bow — sit in the gunner seat to man it.
🎯 Aim within an arc with your mouse; the barrel swings to follow, camera locks to the gun.
💥 It hits WAY harder than a handheld — the boat’s real firepower.
📦 It burns ammo from your cargo, so keep looting ammo crates.
❤️ Enemies now show a health bar so you can see how close they are to dying.
🎨 Greybox turret for now; real gun art + muzzle flash later.
