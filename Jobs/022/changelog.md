🪙 Dev Notes — Runs now pay Gold

🏁 Finishing a run pays the big Gold reward — the further you get, the more you earn.
📉 Even a run that ends early pays out (25/50/75% checkpoints), so no run is wasted…
🥇 …but crossing the finish line pays a premium, so it's always worth pushing to the end.
✨ Rare gold nuggets can turn up at jungle camps — grab them for a little extra.
💰 Your reward is shown on the results screen and added to your Gold balance.
