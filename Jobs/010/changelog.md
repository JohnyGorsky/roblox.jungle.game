🛠️ Dev Notes — Fuel & docks

⛽ The boat burns fuel as you ride — run dry and the engine cuts, leaving you drifting (and swarmed).
📊 New HUD shows your FUEL and BOAT health at a glance.
🛟 Fuel docks now dot the river — pull up alongside one and hold to refuel.
🗺️ Docks are seeded, so the same run always has them in the same spots.
🎨 Greybox docks (plank + drum) for now; proper docks, fuel cans to haul, and shops come later.
