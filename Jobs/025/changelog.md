🚤 Dev Notes — Build up your boat

🛠️ New "Boat Upgrades" shop in the lobby — spend Gold on permanent boat modules.
⚙️ Twin Motors (faster), Reinforced Hull (tougher), Extended Fuel Tank (fewer stops),
   Searchlight (see at night), Cargo Trailer (carry more), Mounted Gun Upgrade.
👀 Every upgrade is a real part you can SEE bolted onto the boat.
🤝 Co-op perk: if anyone on the crew owns a module, the whole boat gets it.
💾 Upgrades are permanent and saved to your profile.
