🛠️ Dev Notes — Weapons loadout & inventory

🎒 New 4-slot inventory — one item active at a time. Tap a slot to switch (mobile-friendly hotbar).
🗡️ Everyone starts with a Sword — your always-there fallback. Short range only: get in close.
🔫 Guns are earned! Loot a weapon crate at a jungle camp to grab a Pistol or Shotgun.
🎯 Each gun has its OWN ammo — top it up from ammo crates at camps. Out of ammo = no shots.
💪 Keep your looted guns for the whole run — going down and getting revived won't lose them.
📦 Carry gasoline/metal back to the boat as before — hands full means no weapon until you drop it.
🎨 Greybox weapons for now; swing animations, real gun art, and tuning come later.
