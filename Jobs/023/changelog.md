💵 Dev Notes — Salvage & the dock shop

🪙 New in-run currency: SALVAGE — earn it as you travel and by looting jungle camps.
🏪 Pull up to a dock and open the DOCK SHOP to spend it.
🩹 Buy bandages, fuel, ammo, repair kits, and weapons (pistol / shotgun).
⚖️ Salvage is per-run only — it resets each run, so spend it before you finish!
