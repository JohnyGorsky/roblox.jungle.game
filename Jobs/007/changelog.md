🛠️ Dev Notes — Croc packs & shooting rules

🐊 Crocs now keep coming — they spawn ahead as you travel and swarm the boat (up to 4 at once).
📈 The further you go, the more often they come (spawn rate ramps up with distance).
🌊 Outrun a croc and it gives up and sinks away; new ones take its place.
🎯 Shooting rules: you can only hit crocs within range, and only if you're free —
   the driver can't shoot, and neither can someone busy catching fish.
🔒 Still all server-checked. Greybox crocs for now; real gators + guns come with the art pass.
