🛠️ Dev Notes — Run endings, results & rewards

🏁 Runs now END: reach the river's end to WIN, or lose the whole crew (or the boat) to fail.
💀 During a run there's no respawn — if you bleed out and no teammate revives you, you're out for the run.
👀 Downed for good? You spectate the rest of the crew's run.
📊 Results screen at the end: win/lose, distance travelled, survivors, and your reward.
💰 Reward scales with distance — even a run that ends early pays out for how far you got.
🔁 Back to the lobby afterwards (button, or automatically) to team up and go again.
