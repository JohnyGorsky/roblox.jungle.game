🛠️ Dev Notes — Lobby & party launch

🛫 New LOBBY: you now start at a departure airfield, not straight in the river run.
🟧 Step on a LAUNCH PAD to form a party — up to 6 players; three pads for three groups.
👑 First on a pad is the leader; the sign shows who's aboard (★ = leader).
🚀 Leader holds START → a short countdown → the whole party launches together into a private run.
🌊 Everyone in a party shares the same river.
🎨 Airfield is rough greybox for now — a proper hand-built hub comes later.
