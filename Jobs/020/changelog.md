📋 Dev Notes — Economy & progression design (planning)

💰 Designed the full money system: earn rare Gold by finishing runs, spend it in the lobby.
🛠️ A 10-skill upgrade tree (1→10) — boat speed, turning, fuel, armor, repair and more.
🚤 Your boat visibly grows: unlock motors, armor, searchlight, trailer and a bigger gun.
🏆 Player ranks (Castaway → River Legend) shown on your head in the lobby + global leaderboards.
🎯 Daily seeded runs, daily rewards and weekly challenges to keep the crew coming back.
🤝 Fair by design — Robux buys looks & convenience, never power.
