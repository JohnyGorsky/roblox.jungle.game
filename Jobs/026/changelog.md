🏆 Dev Notes — Ranks & leaderboards

📈 Every run now builds your lifetime River Score (distance + zones reached + finishing).
🎖️ Climb 10 ranks: Castaway → Drifter → … → River Legend (with prestige stars beyond).
🪧 Your rank floats above your head in the lobby, in your tier's colour, for everyone to see.
🌍 A global Top-10 leaderboard board in the lobby shows the best River Legends.
💾 Rank never goes down and is saved to your profile.
