🌟 Dev Notes — Skill tree

📊 Spend Gold in the lobby on a 10-skill tree — each skill levels 1→10.
🚤 Boat skills tune your ride: top speed, turning, hull HP, fuel efficiency, cargo capacity.
🤝 Crew skills: repair, refuel, revive, reload, and scavenging (effects coming online progressively).
🔧 Skills stack on top of your boat modules and are saved to your profile.
📈 Costs climb per level, so maxing a skill is a real long-term goal.
