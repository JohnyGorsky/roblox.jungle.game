🪨 Dev Notes — Watch the river!

⚠️ River obstacles are now real hazards — weave around them or pay the price.
🪨 Rocks nearly stop you and hit hard. 🪵 Logs slow you and hurt. 🏝️ Sandbars bog you down.
💥 Ram one and your boat loses speed and takes hull damage — the driver's line matters now.
⚓ Tied up safe at a dock? Obstacles won't touch you.
